About Me

Profile

I am Aisheeya Lizabhelle L. Cariaga, a 19-year-old first-year Computer Science student at Laguna State Polytechnic University. I have a strong passion for both technology and creativity, which allows me to approach projects with a balance of logic and artistic expression. I enjoy exploring how design and functionality work together to create meaningful digital experiences. As a student, I am committed to continuous learning and self-improvement, especially in the fields of programming and digital design. I value discipline, focus, and consistency in both my academic and creative work. I am motivated by challenges that push me to think critically and creatively. Through my projects, I aim to build solutions that are not only functional but also visually intentional.

Interests

I have a strong interest in digital design, particularly creating clean, visually appealing layouts with a clear purpose. I enjoy writing, as it allows me to express ideas and creativity in a more reflective way. Arts and crafts also play a role in my creative process, helping me develop patience and attention to detail. Alongside creative interests, I am passionate about coding and learning how systems and applications are built. I enjoy exploring the intersection of design and technology. In my free time, I stay active through sports, especially table tennis, which helps improve my focus and coordination. These interests reflect my balanced approach to creativity, logic, and personal growth.

Skills

I have developed strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, allowing me to analyze situations and find efficient solutions. I am naturally creative, which helps me generate ideas that are both innovative and visually engaging. My adaptability enables me to learn new tools and adjust quickly to different tasks and environments. I also demonstrate leadership skills, particularly in group projects and collaborative settings. I possess solid digital and computer skills, which I apply in design, programming, and academic work. I work well independently but also thrive in team-based environments. Overall, I approach tasks with focus, responsibility, and attention to detail.

Experience

I have nearly two years of experience working as a Canva graphic designer, where I design wedding invitations and website layouts as a side gig. This experience has strengthened my creativity, time management, and ability to meet client expectations. I have worked on various design styles, allowing me to adapt to different themes and preferences. I am also a graphic committee member of the Organization for Computing and Technology Advancement (OCTA) at Laguna State Polytechnic University. In this role, I contribute to visual materials for events, announcements, and organizational branding. Additionally, I have completed multiple school-based design projects that required collaboration and technical understanding. These experiences have helped me build confidence in both my creative and technical abilities.
